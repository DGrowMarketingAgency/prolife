Just Pregnant

8–10 Weeks: Time for Your First Visit

You have missed your period and the stick reads positive… Congratulations! Regardless of how experienced or how raw, you may feel excitement, joy, anxiety, uncertainty or fear — and that’s completely normal.

SEE HOW WE CAN HELP YOU

Now is the time to contact us so together we can plan for the months ahead. We will ensure you are prepared as best as possible, both physically and emotionally, so you can embrace the surprises and joys to come.

First Visit (8–10 Weeks)

We’d love to meet you and your partner around 8–10 weeks, earlier if you have any concerns or past complications. This session lasts 45–60 minutes and includes an in-depth discussion and planning for your pregnancy journey.

Comprehensive Check-Up

We’ll record your blood pressure, height, and weight, and perform an ultrasound scan to confirm your pregnancy dates and ensure viability.

Screening Tests

Based on your situation, we’ll guide you through optional screening for chromosomal abnormalities and diagnostic testing if required.

Emotional Wellbeing

We’ll discuss your emotional state and provide guidance to help you feel confident and supported throughout your pregnancy.
