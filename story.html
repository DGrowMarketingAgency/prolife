Our Story

A health organization since December 2013 serving the people in and around Manapakkam in Chennai. Trusted and supported by ladies in Manapakkam, Mugalivakkam, Porur, Ramapuram, etc and IT parks such as but not limited to DLF, HP, L & T, Vishranth Tech Par, Olympia Tech Park, Tamarai Tech Park, etc.

Consultations, Scans, OG Procedures such as but not limited to Copper T, Laparoscopy, Hysteroscopy, Uterus removal, endometrial ablation, uterine fibroids, vulvectomy, Cervical cerclage, Pap Smear, Ectopic pregnancy, Menopause management, PCOS/PCOD, Breast screening, Cancer screening, Dilation and curettage (D&C), Medical termination etc being carried out in the clinic. Delivery being conducted to the patient choice in accordance to the nearest geographic location, economical condition, claim eligibility, etc. In a short span hundreds of infertility cases had been successfully treated with the positive result.

More than a clinic and less than a hospital with all facility right from Consultation, Scan, Lab and Medicine under one roof. This ensures patients getting the scan done at the consultation place itself rather than consulting at one place and scanning at different place by different person which makes them to spend more time and travel.

YOUR PREGNANCY. YOUR BIRTH. YOUR OBSTETRICIAN.

Your chosen Obstetrician will provide you with a personalised experience and individualised care, with the back up of like-minded, skilled practitioners, our cover arrangements make it most likely that your Obstetrician will be there all the way.

HIGH RISK WOMEN’S HEALTH CARE

Located within the safe confines of near by Hospital, you have direct access to the highest level of private maternal and neonatal intensive care available. Being onsite means we are on hand, to be there when you need us most.

ADVANCED ULTRASOUND TECHNOLOGY

Using the latest ultrasound technology, your baby will be closely monitored throughout your pregnancy, allowing you to see and hear them at every stage.

TRUST IN EXPERIENCE

Building on over 20 years of experience, our team of women’s health specialists collaborate, to provide you with the highest level of care.

SPECIALISED WOMEN’S HEALTH PHYSIOTHERAPIST

Working in close conjunction with an experienced, specialist women’s health and pelvic floor physiotherapist, our goal is protection and prevention, keeping you strong and comfortable, at every age.

MIDWIFERY AND BREASTFEEDING SERVICES

Our midwife practices alongside your chosen Obstetrician, building on discussions about your pregnancy, labour, birth expectations and plans, providing the additional care and understanding needed to help support and smooth the transition to your new role as mother.
